Entropy Games Privacy Policy

Effective Date and Last Updated: November 28, 2021

What data do we collect?

  • Username
  • Email
  • Current Settings
  • Data with ESPM (Entropy Script Particle Manager):
    • Packages you have downloaded (date, name)
    • Packages you have published (data in the particle.json file)

How do we collect your data?

  • Interacting with https://entropygames.io
  • Using the ESPM CLI with the Entropy Script NPM package

3rd Parties

  • Google Account: when you sign in with Google

How do we store your data?

Most of the data is stored in a MySQL database hosted on entropygames.io.
Sensitive data (passwords) are encrypted.

What are your data protection rights?

Entropy Games would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access – You have the right to request Entropy Games for copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification – You have the right to request that Entropy Games correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Entropy Games to complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure – You have the right to request that Entropy Games erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that Entropy Games restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to Entropy Games processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability – You have the right to request that Entropy Games transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

What log data do we collect?

We collect anonymous data whenever you visit entropygames.io like the time and date.
This data doesn't not contain any personally identifiable information.
